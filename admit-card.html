About Admit Card 2026

An admit card (also known as hall ticket or examination ticket) is an important document that you must carry to the examination center. It contains essential information like your roll number, exam date, exam time, exam center address, and other important instructions.

How to Download Admit Card?

  1. Visit the official website of the examination board
  2. Look for "Download Admit Card" section
  3. Enter your registration number/roll number and date of birth
  4. Click on the download button
  5. Print the admit card or save it as PDF
  6. Keep multiple copies for future reference

Important Things to Check on Your Admit Card

  • Your name and date of birth
  • Roll number and registration number
  • Exam date and time
  • Exam center address and code
  • Subject/stream information
  • Photograph and signature quality

What to Carry on Exam Day?

  • Original admit card (printed)
  • Valid photo ID proof (Aadhar, Passport, Driving License, etc.)
  • Pen/pencil as per exam requirements
  • Calculator (if allowed)
  • Water bottle
  • Any other item specified in admit card
